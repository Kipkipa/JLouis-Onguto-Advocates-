About Us

Jlouis Onguto Advocates is a dynamic law firm headquartered in Nairobi's Upper Hill district. We provide decisive, pragmatic, and highly structured legal solutions. Our practice is built on precision, rigorous legal research, and an uncompromising commitment to protecting our clients' strategic interests.

Practice Areas

Civil Litigation

Robust representation across all judicial tiers, from the Small Claims Court to complex appellate reviews, backed by meticulous research and tactical advocacy.

Commercial Law

Drafting, reviewing, and structuring complex commercial agreements, ensuring corporate compliance, and providing strategic advisory for modern businesses.

Probate & Administration

Compassionate and comprehensive legal guidance in estate planning, the creation of trusts and wills, and the seamless execution of probate matters.

Conveyancing

Comprehensive legal mechanics for commercial and residential property transactions, land use planning, title transfers, and development financing.
