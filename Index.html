About Us

J. Louis Onguto Advocates is a dynamic commercial law firm headquartered in Nairobi's Upper Hill district. We provide decisive, pragmatic, and highly structured legal solutions for corporate and private clients. Our practice is built on commercial precision, rigorous legal research, and an uncompromising commitment to protecting our clients' strategic interests.

Practice Areas

Commercial & Corporate Law

Drafting, reviewing, and structuring complex commercial agreements—from customized service contracts to major corporate structuring and advisory.

Civil & Commercial Litigation

Robust representation across all judicial tiers, from the Small Claims Court to complex appellate reviews, backed by meticulous research and strategic advocacy.

Real Estate & Conveyancing

Comprehensive legal mechanics for commercial property transactions, land use, and development financing.

Alternative Dispute Resolution

Navigating mediation and arbitration to achieve swift, confidential, and commercially viable settlements outside the courtroom.
